Christians Business Community and Workers Conference 2024

Introduction

Tanzania Christian Volunteer Service (CVS) is a local Christian organization registered on 15th January 1992 under the Societies Act Cap 337 R.E 2002 with Registration Certificate No. S.A.7478. Official operations in Kigoma/Ujiji Municipality began in 2005. CVS serves as a local religious NGO across regions in Tanzania including Tanga, Morogoro, Dar-Es-Salaam, and Zanzibar.

Conference Goal

To challenge and equip Christian businesspeople and employees to integrate Biblical values and spiritual gifts in fulfilling the Kingdom of Heaven's purpose on Earth.

Objectives

  • Remind participants of God's blessings (Proverbs 28:20, Ephesians 1:3, Deut. 28:2).
  • Honor God through hard work and faithfulness (Matthew 5:6, 2 Thes. 3:10).
  • Receive wisdom and spiritual power for business growth (Ephesians 1:17).
  • Encourage thanksgiving, prayer, and giving back (1 Chronicles 16:8, 2 Corinthians 9:7).
  • Minister healing and deliverance (John 2:11).

Proceedings

  • 4-day conference at Christian Hall.
  • Participants from Kigoma, Uvinza, and Kasulu districts.
  • Speakers and ministries providing evangelical insight.
  • Focus Discussion Groups and fundraising activities.

Target Participants

Employees, enterprise owners, service providers, farmers, pastors, ministries, etc., totaling 71 participants.

Presenter Qualifications

  • Experienced in Christian business conferences and spiritually inspired presentations.
  • Skilled in presentation tools and software.
  • Strong spiritual background, professional in business or economics (optional).

Expected Outcomes

  • Understanding God's role in business success.
  • Empowered with Holy Spirit to grow in ministry and business.
  • Encouraged to give back and support community evangelism.

Accelerative Way

  • Commitment to Kingdom advancement and community service.
  • Continual encouragement to Christian peers.
